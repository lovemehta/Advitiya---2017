Rules for Accommodation

1.Production of College ID card is mandatory at the Registration Desk.

2. After successful registration, each registered student would be given an Accommodation Pass. It is mandatory for all registered students to carry this pass throughout their stay during the fest.

3. Accommodation would be strictly on first-come-first-serve and shared basis.

4. Mattresses, blankets and bed sheets will be provided during your accommodation.

5. Advitiya, IIT Ropar does not bear any responsibility for any material loss during your stay at the campus.

6. Accommodation fee is exclusive of any food charges. You can avail food facility at the food stalls on payment basis.

7. If any student is found responsible for causing damage to the property of IIT Ropar, the concerned student will be liable to the full cost of replacement/repair.
